What VisionComfort includes

Three main tools, kept simple so you can focus on reading and comfort, not configuration.

  • Vision Test Flow – quick calibration to estimate your comfortable reading size.
  • Web Reader – open article links in a cleaner, adjustable view with a text size slider.
  • Games Hub – light mini games (Contrast Hunter, Saccade Trainer, TextZoom challenge) focused on comfort, not speed-running.
  • Local settings only – your preferences stay on your device; no cloud accounts.
  • Small, static banners – one ad banner per screen, no full-screen surprises.
