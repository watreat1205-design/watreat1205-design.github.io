VisionVideo Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

VisionVideo respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the VisionVideo mobile application handles information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

VisionVideo does not require users to create an account and does not collect, store, or share personal account information.

The app allows users to select photos, videos, music, and audio files from their own device in order to create story videos. These files are used locally inside the app for editing and exporting videos.

Media and User Content

Photos, video clips, captions, voice recordings, music files, and exported videos remain on the user’s device. VisionVideo does not upload user media or exported videos to our own servers.

Microphone Permission

VisionVideo may request microphone access so users can record narration or voice-over audio for their videos. Voice recordings are saved locally on the device and used only for the user’s video project.

Advertising

VisionVideo may show rewarded ads using Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and process certain device, advertising, and usage information according to Google’s own privacy policies.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Data Sharing

VisionVideo does not sell personal data. We do not share user-created videos, photos, captions, or recordings with third parties.

Children

VisionVideo is not specifically directed at children. Users should only use the app in accordance with the age requirements and policies of Google Play.

Security

Because user projects and exported videos are stored locally on the device, users are responsible for managing and protecting their own device and files.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
